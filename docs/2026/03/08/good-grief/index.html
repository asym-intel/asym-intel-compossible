Good Grief

AI generated image

My son

One day you’ll learn

That love’s a kind of grief

Delayed

A journey with

The ferryman

Who must be paid

You’ll learn to hold

Love’s razor

Sharp blade

Just know

Each cut

Lets out

Your tender heart
