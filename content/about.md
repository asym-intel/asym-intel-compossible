Compossible is a word coined by Leibniz to describe things that can coexist — that are mutually possible. It is a blog about ideas, strategy, and the philosophy of the present, written from Gibraltar.

The writing covers European geopolitics and strategy, AI governance and ethics, democratic integrity, economics and inequality, and — when the mood takes me — poetry and music.

**Peter Howitt** is CEO of [Ramparts.gi](https://ramparts.gi), a Gibraltar-based law and advisory firm specialising in financial services, AI governance, and regulatory compliance. He also publishes [Asymmetric Intelligence](https://asym-intel.info), a public intelligence commons.
