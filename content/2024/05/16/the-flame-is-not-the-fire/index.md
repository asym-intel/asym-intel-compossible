_In memory of Gerard Manley Hopkins_

* * *

Today I see the god 

In everything

And everything to me 

Proclaims and sings

And dances

Like the dragonflies

In spring

* * *

Gentle reminder 

That the flame 

Is not the fire

Love every host

And every ghost 

That lives within

* * *
